Imperative

-is a verb in the base form
-usually does not have a subject

used to give
*orders/instruction
*encourage someone
*suggest something
*offer something
*express wishes for someone's welfare

-affirmative
  go to school
  eat your lunch
  have a good rest
  let me do this alone
  let's go to the movies

-negative
  dont run near to the pool
  dont waste water
  dont go to bed too late
  dont hurt the dog
  dont ask me about the test now
